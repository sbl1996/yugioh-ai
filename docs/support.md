# Summon

## Tribute Summon
Through `select_tribute` (multi-select)

## Link Summon
Through `select_unselect_card` (select 1 card per time)

## Syncro Summon
- `select_card` to choose the tuner (usually 1 card)
- `select_sum` to choose the non-tuner (1 card per time)
